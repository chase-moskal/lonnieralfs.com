Athena

20 hours

Athena is a figurine I created for Andrew Fudge for his boardgame project "Godlike"

She's a formidable warrior who has a pretty cool shield decorated with a Gorgon's head

I based her armor design on Greek and Celtic symbols. She has Zeus' lightning bolt on her back

I'm happy about how the skirt and cape turned out, I've been using Marvelous Designer to simulate clothing

Sobek the Egyptian Crocodile God

14 hours

Sobek is a figurine I've sculpted for Andrew Fudge's boardgame project "Godlike"

Sobek is the God of the Nile, and he does not take kindly to trespassers

He has thick and scaly skin, and is decked out with Egyptian-style armor

☥His necklace has the "Ankh" symbol which means Life

Sobek also has an Eye of Horus on his belt, which is a sign of royal power and good health
