Sobek the Egyptian Crocodile God

Andrew came to me with his vision of an Egyptian God, Sobek, who is half-man and half-crocodile! It was really fun making his scaley skin, his Egyptian armor, and giving him a "coming at you" kind of pose

I came up with the idea to use a real hieroglyph, "Ankh", which means "eternal life". Andrew liked it, so we put it in Sobek's neckpiece. We added a few more too

This is for a game Andrew is working on called "Godlike", check it out!
